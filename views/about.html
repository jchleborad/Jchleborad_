About Me and this Site

John & Cindy


Hi, I’m John Chleborad – welcome to my corner of the web.

I'm a husband, father, builder, coder, and lifelong learner. My wife, Cindy, and I love exploring the State of Arizona and other parts of the Country together. When presented with a choice, we prefer to drive rather than fly—there’s something special about the time we spend together watching the landscape unfold mile by mile. It’s not just about the destination for us; it’s about the journey.

Family is everything. We’ve been blessed with two amazing children (now young adults), Danyel and Tyler, and nothing brings us more joy than spending time with them and watching them grow into the wonderful people they are.

I like to stay active and creative. I enjoy cycling, golfing, and working with my hands—whether that’s in the shop, building things with wood, crafting leather goods, or tinkering with new ideas, I’ve always had an appreciatation for hand-made items.

Building this website was a personal milestone. After earning a Software Development Certification through WOZ U’s coding bootcamp, I set out to create something of my own—something that reflects my journey, skills, and passions. Watching this site come together from scratch has been one of the most rewarding parts of my journey.

In my early life I played baseball. I pitched for Rapid City Post 22 , represented USA Baseball , earning a Silver Medal in the 1983 World Friendship Series, and played at San Diego State University—GO AZTECS! That time taught me discipline, resilience, and teamwork—values I still carry with me every day.

Thanks for stopping by and getting to know me a little better. I hope this site gives you a glimpse of who I am and what I care about.
